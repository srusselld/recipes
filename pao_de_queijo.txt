Ingredients:
 - 1/2 cup butter
 - 1/3 cup water
 - 1/3 cup milk
 - pinch of salt
 - 2 cloves garlic, minced (or more)
 - 2 cups povilho (cassava/tapioca flour)
 - 2/3 cups shredded cheese (either queijo minas or monterrey jack)
 - 1 egg

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and butter up a pan
 2. Combine butter, water, milk, salt and heat until butter melts
 3. Add minced garlic and povilho, roll dough into ball
 4. Let dough rest for 15 minutes
 5. Add shredded cheese and egg
 6. Butter up your hands and roll into pao de queijo balls
 7. Bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes (until they are very lightly brown)
